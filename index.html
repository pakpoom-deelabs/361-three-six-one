— Our Story —

Where a family rice mill
became a Nordic landmark.

361 THREE SIX ONE is a quiet conversation between two worlds. The structure stands on the soil of a family teak wood rice mill — a place where generations once stored harvests and shared meals. Today, that same timber breathes through a Modern Nordic silhouette: black-stained walls, warm wood grain, and slow, honest light.

Spanning more than 7 Rai, our home is built for stillness and gathering — a destination for coffee lovers, families, and travelers chasing a moment worth keeping.
